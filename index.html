ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Edison High School's Boys Beach Volleyball program is one of the top club programs in Orange County. The team competes in the Orange County Volleyball Beach League (OCVBL), a club sport featuring some of the most competitive schools in Southern California. This is not a CIF program.

The program runs as a fall sport, typically beginning the week before school starts and running through playoffs in mid-to-late October. Athletes develop the serving, passing, setting, and competitive instincts needed to go up against Orange County's best and earn their spot at the next level.

The team practices at Newland Beach Volleyball Courts in Huntington Beach, right in the heart of Surf City USA, the same sand that has produced some of the country's finest beach volleyball players.
